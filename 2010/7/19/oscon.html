Update: OSCON was a lot of fun... if anyone is interested in seeing the slides Alex and I wrote they’re up on slide share.

I won’t be blogging here, but if you’re interested in what’s going on at OSCON follow me on Twitter @pat_shaughnessy, or the official tag is #OSCON. If you are in Portland definitely drop by and say hello!
